HOW TECHNLOGY HAS
AFFECTED US IN OUR
DAILY LIFE?


What is Technology?

Technology can be described as the use of knowledge in the design and production of devices and equipment that can be used in a variety of ways. For each and every one of us, technology has become an indispensable part of our daily lives. Despite the fact that technology has made life easier for all of us.Technology has existed since the beginning of time. We have always had different forms of technology at different points in our civilisation as humans.


Technological impact on human life

Learning is one aspect of technology that has a significant impact on society. It has become more interactive and collaborative, allowing people to better engage with the material they are learning and having difficulty with. Students can take quizzes and exams more easily, and teachers can hold more effective online classes. It also pushes the classroom's boundaries, encouraging self-paced learning. It can alse be accessed throght different social media platforms. This is more beneficial to student's learning than sitting in lectures and reading textbooks. Learning has become easier as a result of technological advancements.

Similarly, Comunication is the huge impact in our live that technology has made.Many new forms of electronic communication have emerged as a result of technological advancements. For example, there are emails, social networking, the ability to facetime someone on the other side of the world, and video conferencing, which allows you to hold virtual meetings.

Finally, technological advancements in the health industry have aided in keeping people safe and healthy. There are numerous innovative phone apps that allow people to keep track of their weight, calorie intake,nheart rate, and other health metrics at any time of day.

However, surveys indicate that mobile media has a negative impact on people's ability to be sociable and make face-to-face conversation. Mobile technology has the potential to reduce interpersonal contact and relationships. There is less free time, and you may find that you do not have enough time for yourself when you are always in touch with others. It may also be a source of distraction from your studies.

What makes technology good or bad for humans?

Although there are several examples of how technology has impacted our lives, there are a few that may be termed revolutions for humanity. One such example is the technology employed in the field of robotics. People and companies all around the globe are now using robots for a variety of purposes.They're also deployed in regions that might  be dangerous to people, i.e safeguarding us. Cleaning and disposal of radioactive waste is one of these areas. Another sector that is a great illustration of man's technical accomplishments is Artificial Intelligence (AI). It's a machine that's intelligent is enough to act like us. AI can carryout the operations such as problem-solving, planning, learning, and even thinking. AI is now employed in almost every major industry, including defense, security, healthcare, and communication. Therefore, we can say that Technology has made is really good impact in human's life.

But in the other hand, some people are deeply opposed to technology, claiming that it will lead to the extinction of ‘humanity'and that we are on the verge of a world where robots would govern everything. Therefore, we can assume that technology can turn out bad on human life.
